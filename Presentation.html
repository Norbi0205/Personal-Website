Presentation

I consider that my most important quality is ambition. I like to see every task completed and I don't give up regardless of the situation. Even as a child I had this way of thinking, I always aimed for bigger goals and I had to win. I can say that intrinsic motivation and this mindset played an essential role in my formation and thus I became the person I am now. I learned that you have to be fair, dignified, persistent and optimistic. Moreover , I am a punctual, focused, creative and organized person. I have successfully passed the challenges so far and I am willing to pass the next ones as well, because I see them as steps of initiation. I learn quickly and I like logical thinking, that is why I chose the Technical University of Cluj Napoca. My passions are programming, electronics and telecommunications and I would like to develop in these fields, because in my opinion they suit me best. Precisely for this reason, I enrolled in the mathematics-informatics high school and decided to pursue this field further, choosing the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology.
